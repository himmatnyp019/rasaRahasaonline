3. Defined Terms

A
Account
A user profile created on the RASA RAHASA platform that allows access to Services. An Account includes login credentials (username, email, and password), personal information, order history, and preferences. Users may create multiple Accounts, but each must use a unique email address.
Address
The physical delivery location provided by the User for order fulfillment. This includes street address, building name/number, apartment/unit number, city, postal code, and any special delivery instructions. Accurate Address information is critical for successful delivery.
Appeal
A formal request by a User to reconsider or overturn a penalty, suspension, or termination decision. Appeals can be submitted through the Help Center, email, or phone within 30 days of the penalty being applied.
Authentication
The process of verifying User identity through login credentials (username/email and password). Authentication ensures secure access to User Accounts and protects against unauthorized access.
B
Banned Account
An Account that has been suspended or permanently terminated due to violation of Terms of Use or Guidelines. Banned Accounts have restricted or no access to platform Services, depending on the type of ban (temporary or permanent).
Business Days
Monday through Friday, excluding South Korean national holidays and Company-designated holidays. Business Days are used to calculate processing times, refund periods, and response times.
C
Company / We / Our / Us
Refers to RASA RAHASA SDS, a foreign mart operating online and offline shopping platforms in South Korea, with physical retail locations in Busan.
Content
Any text, images, reviews, messages, comments, or other materials posted, uploaded, or transmitted by Users on the Platform. Content includes product reviews, chat messages, profile information, and any user-generated material.
CRUD Operations
Create, Read, Update, and Delete operations that Users can perform on their personal data. This represents the full control Users have over their information stored on the Platform.
Customer / Customer User
A User who makes purchases through the Platform, either online or offline. Customers engage in transactions and receive Products through our Services.
D
Data / Personal Data
Any information relating to an identified or identifiable User, including but not limited to: username, email address, phone number, physical address, login time, profile image, order history, payment information, and communication records.
DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service)
A cyber attack that attempts to disrupt normal traffic and operations of a platform by overwhelming it with a flood of internet traffic. Participation in DDoS attacks is a severe violation resulting in permanent account termination.
Delivery
The process of transporting and delivering ordered Products from Company warehouses or retail locations to the User's specified Address. Delivery includes the physical handover of Products to the User or designated recipient.
Delivery Company / Delivery Partner
Third-party courier or logistics companies contracted by RASA RAHASA SDS to deliver Products to Customers. These partners receive limited User information (name, address, phone number) solely for delivery purposes.
Dispute
Any disagreement, complaint, or conflict between the Company and a User regarding Services, Products, Orders, policies, or any aspect of the User-Company relationship.
E
Email Address
A unique electronic mail address provided by the User for account registration, authentication, and communication purposes. Each Account must have a unique email address.
Encryption
The process of encoding information to protect it from unauthorized access. The Company uses encryption for storing User passwords and protecting sensitive data during transmission.
Expiration Date / Expired Product
The date after which a Product is no longer safe or suitable for consumption or use. All Products sold by RASA RAHASA SDS have expiration dates. Delivery of Expired Products entitles Users to free exchange and additional compensation.
F
Force Majeure
Unforeseeable circumstances that prevent the Company from fulfilling its obligations, including: natural disasters, wars, government actions, pandemics, network failures, or other events beyond reasonable control.
Fraud / Fraudulent Activity
Intentional deception or misrepresentation for personal gain or to cause harm to others, including: using fake names, payment fraud, identity theft, or manipulation of Platform systems.
G
Guidelines
The community standards, rules, and behavioral expectations set forth in our Terms of Use and Guidelines Violation Policy that govern acceptable conduct on the Platform.
H
Hacking
Unauthorized access, modification, or exploitation of Platform systems, code, databases, or User accounts. Hacking is a severe violation resulting in immediate permanent termination and potential legal action.
Harassment
Unwelcome conduct that creates an intimidating, hostile, or offensive environment, including: threats, sexual harassment, hate speech, stalking, or persistent unwanted communication.
Help Center
A dedicated support section of the Platform accessible to all Users (including banned Users) that provides: policy information, reporting tools, appeal submission forms, FAQs, and customer support access.
I
Intellectual Property
Trademarks, logos, business names, proprietary processes, designs, copyrights, and other protected materials owned by RASA RAHASA SDS or licensed for its use.
L
Login Credentials
The username (or email) and password combination used to authenticate and access a User Account. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their Login Credentials.
Login Time
The timestamp recorded when a User successfully authenticates and accesses their Account. Login Time is tracked for security purposes and account activity monitoring.
M
Minor Violation
A less serious infraction of Platform rules including: using fraudulent names, mild inappropriate language, or minor spam. Minor Violations result in warnings and 1-day temporary suspension.
Mobile Application / App
The RASA RAHASA mobile software application available for smartphones and tablets that provides access to Platform Services.
Moderate Violation
A significant infraction of Platform rules including: rude communication in chat or reviews, harassment, or content violations. Moderate Violations result in immediate content removal and 1-week suspension.
N
Notification
Communication sent to Users regarding: account activity, order updates, violations, policy changes, or other important information. Notifications are sent via email, platform messages, and/or dialog boxes.
O
Offline Store / Retail Location
Physical brick-and-mortar locations operated by RASA RAHASA SDS in South Korea where Asian products are sold directly to customers.
Order
A request placed by a User to purchase one or more Products through the Platform. Orders include: product details, quantities, pricing, delivery information, and payment details.
Order Cancellation
The termination of an Order before or after processing, which may be initiated by the User or Company under specific circumstances outlined in the Terms of Use.
P
Package
The physical container(s) in which Products are shipped and delivered to Users. Packages may contain one or multiple Products from an Order.
Password
A confidential string of characters used for authentication and account security. Passwords are stored in encrypted format and should never be shared with others.
Payment Information / Payment Data
Information related to financial transactions including: payment receipts, transaction proofs, payment method details. The Company does NOT store credit card numbers, PINs, or complete payment card details.
Payment Method
The means by which Users pay for Orders, including: South Korean bank cards, Visa cards, MasterCard, and other accepted payment instruments.
Payment Processor
Third-party financial service providers that handle payment transactions on behalf of RASA RAHASA SDS. These processors receive limited User information (email) for receipt delivery and verification.
Penalty
A consequence imposed for violation of Platform Guidelines, including: warnings, content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent termination.
Personal Information
See "Data / Personal Data" definition.
Phone Number
A contact telephone number provided by Users for order updates, delivery coordination, and important account notifications. Accurate Phone Numbers are essential for successful service delivery.
Platform
The entire RASA RAHASA SDS ecosystem including: website, mobile application, offline stores, and all related services and infrastructure.
Privacy Policy
The policy document that explains how RASA RAHASA SDS collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects User Personal Data.
Product(s)
Asian groceries, food items, and other merchandise sold by RASA RAHASA SDS through online and offline channels. All Products are perishable goods with expiration dates.
Profile / User Profile
The collection of information associated with a User Account, including: username, profile image, contact information, preferences, and order history.
Profile Image / Profile Picture
An optional image uploaded by Users to personalize their Account. Profile Images must comply with content guidelines and cannot contain inappropriate, sexual, or offensive material.
Prohibited Activity / Prohibited Use
Any action or behavior that violates Platform Guidelines, including but not limited to: hacking, data theft, harassment, fraud, inappropriate content, or system manipulation.
R
Receipt
A document (electronic or physical) that confirms a completed transaction, including: order details, payment amount, payment method, date, and transaction ID.
Refund
The return of payment to a User due to: order cancellation, delivery of wrong/expired/damaged Products, or other circumstances outlined in the Refund Policy.
Registration
The process of creating a User Account on the Platform by providing required information (username, email, password, phone number, address).
Report / Reporting
The act of notifying the Company about violations, inappropriate conduct, or policy breaches by other Users. Reporting can be done anonymously or with identification.
Review / Product Review
User-generated Content that provides feedback, opinions, or ratings about purchased Products. Reviews are publicly displayed and must comply with content guidelines.
Risk Transfer
The point at which responsibility for Product safety and condition transfers from the Company to the User. Risk Transfer occurs upon successful delivery to the User's Address.
S
Service(s)
All features, functions, and offerings provided by RASA RAHASA SDS, including: online shopping platform, mobile app, offline stores, delivery services, customer support, and related services.
Severe Violation
A critical infraction of Platform rules that poses serious security risks or harm, including: hacking, DDoS attacks, data cracking, payment fraud, severe harassment, or threatening behavior. Severe Violations result in immediate permanent termination without warnings.
South Korean Law
The legal framework and regulations of the Republic of Korea that govern the Company's operations, user agreements, and dispute resolution.
Suspension
Temporary restriction of Account access and Services due to violation of Guidelines. Suspensions can be: 1 day (minor violations), 1 week (moderate violations), or permanent (severe violations).
System Security
The technical safeguards, protocols, and measures implemented to protect Platform infrastructure, User data, and service integrity from unauthorized access or attacks.
T
Termination
Permanent closure of a User Account due to severe violations or repeated violations after warnings. Terminated Accounts cannot be restored without successful appeal.
Terms / Terms of Use
The legal agreement that governs the relationship between Users and RASA RAHASA SDS, outlining rights, responsibilities, rules, and conditions for using the Platform.
Third Party / Third-Party
Any entity or person other than the User or RASA RAHASA SDS, including: payment processors, delivery companies, service providers, and other business partners.
Tracking Information
Data provided to Users about their Order's location and delivery status, including: shipping updates, delivery estimates, and courier contact information.
Transaction
Any exchange of Products for payment between a User and RASA RAHASA SDS, including: the Order placement, payment processing, and Product delivery.
U
User / You / Your
Any individual or entity that accesses, uses, or interacts with the RASA RAHASA SDS Platform, Services, or offline stores, whether or not they have created an Account or made a purchase.
Username
A unique identifier chosen by the User during Registration that is used for Account identification and authentication. Usernames must not be fraudulent or impersonate others.
User-Generated Content
See "Content" definition.
V
Violation
Any breach or non-compliance with Platform Guidelines, Terms of Use, or policies. Violations are classified as Minor, Moderate, or Severe based on their nature and impact.
W
Warning
A formal notice issued to a User about a violation of Guidelines. Users receive up to 3 warnings before suspension (except for severe violations which result in immediate termination).
Website
The RASA RAHASA SDS web-based platform accessible through internet browsers, where Users can browse Products, create Accounts, place Orders, and access Services.
