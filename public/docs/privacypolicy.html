1. Introduction and Our Commitment

Welcome to RASA RAHASA SDS ("we," "our," or "us"). We operate an online e-commerce platform and physical retail stores (collectively, the "Service") specializing in Asian food products for a diverse community of customers residing in South Korea. Our mission is to connect you with the flavors you love, and we are equally dedicated to protecting the personal information you share with us.

This comprehensive Privacy Policy has been created to provide you with a clear and detailed understanding of:

  • What personal information we collect from you.
  • Why we collect this information and the legal basis for doing so.
  • How we use, store, and protect your information.
  • With whom we share this information, and under what circumstances.
  • What rights and choices you have regarding your personal data.

By registering for an account, making a purchase, or otherwise using our Service, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, your choice is not to use our Service.

2. Definitions

To ensure clarity, here are some key terms used throughout this document:

  • Personal Information (or Personal Data): Any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, address, email address, phone number, and account username.
  • User ("you" or "your"): Any individual who accesses or uses our Service, including browsing our website, creating an account, or making a purchase online or in-store.
  • Service: The combined offering of our online shopping website, mobile applications (if any), and our physical retail locations in South Korea.
  • Processing: Any operation performed on Personal Information, such as collection, recording, organization, storage, use, disclosure, or deletion.
  • Data Controller: The entity that determines the purposes and means of the processing of Personal Information. For the purposes of this policy, RASA RAHASA SDS is the Data Controller.
  • Third-Party: Any natural or legal person, public authority, agency, or body other than the User or RASA RAHASA SDS.

3. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all Personal Information processed by RASA RAHASA SDS through:

  • Our official website and any future mobile applications.

  • Purchases and interactions at our physical retail locations in South Korea.

4. Information We Collect

We collect information in a few different ways, always with the goal of providing a secure and efficient service. We are committed to collecting only the data that is necessary for our operations.

4.1. Information You Provide to Us Directly

This is information you knowingly and voluntarily provide when you interact with our Service.

  • Account Registration Data: When you create an online account, we collect your Username (to identify your account), Email address (for authentication, communication, and password resets), and your Password (which is immediately encrypted and never stored in plain text).
  • Order and Delivery Data: When you place an order, we collect Your Full Name, a precise Delivery Address in South Korea, and a Contact Phone Number. This is essential for us and our delivery partners to fulfill your order.
  • Profile Personalization Data (Optional): You have the option to upload a Profile Image. This is not required to use our service and is used solely for personalizing your account interface.
  • Customer Support Communications: If you contact us via email or phone, we will collect your name, contact information, and the content of your query to provide assistance and maintain a record of our correspondence.

4.2. Information Related to Your Transactions

While we do not handle your sensitive payment details directly, we do collect information about your transactions.

  • Payment Status: Our system receives a confirmation from our payment partners (PortOne, Kakao Pay) indicating whether a transaction was a success or a failure.
  • Transaction Records: We store a record of the products you purchased, the date and time of the transaction, and the order total. This is necessary for order fulfillment, customer support (e.g., returns or issues), and our own financial accounting.

4.3. Information Collected Automatically

We keep automatic data collection to a minimum to respect your privacy.

  • Login Time and IP Address: For security purposes, we log the time, date, and IP address associated with each login to your account. This helps us detect and prevent unauthorized access and protect your account's integrity.
What We Intentionally DO NOT Collect:

To build trust and protect your privacy, we want to be explicit about the data we avoid collecting:

  • Sensitive Financial Data: We never see, collect, or store your full credit card number, CVV code, or bank account details. This is all handled securely by our payment partners.
  • Tracking Cookies for Advertising: We do not use cookies or similar technologies to track your browsing habits across other websites for the purpose of targeted advertising.
  • Precise Geolocation Data: We do not track your device's real-time location.
  • Device and Browser Fingerprinting: We do not collect detailed information about your browser version, operating system, plugins, or screen resolution for profiling purposes.

Under data protection laws, we are required to have a valid legal basis for processing your Personal Information. We use your data for the following purposes, grounded in the corresponding legal bases:

Much of our data processing is necessary to perform the service you have requested from us.

  • Processing Orders: Using your name, address, and order details to prepare and ship your products.

  • System Maintenance: Using technical logs to diagnose and fix problems with our website.

5.3. To Comply with Legal Obligations

We are subject to South Korean laws and may need to process your data to comply with them.

6. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your Personal Information with third parties except in the limited circumstances described below. We ensure that any third party with access to your data is contractually obligated to keep it confidential and secure.

We work with trusted partners who perform essential functions on our behalf. They are only given access to the information necessary to perform their specific task.

We may be required to disclose your Personal Information in response to a subpoena, court order, or other governmental request, if we believe in good faith that the disclosure is required by law and necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

7. Payment Processing

We are committed to ensuring the security of your payment information. To this end, we have chosen to partner with industry-leading, secure payment gateways.

Our Process:

  1. When you are ready to pay, you are redirected from our website to the secure, encrypted environment of our payment partner (e.g., PortOne or Kakao Pay).
  2. You enter your payment details directly into their system. At no point does this information pass through or get stored on our servers.
  3. They process the transaction and send us a secure notification confirming whether the payment was successful or not, along with a unique transaction identifier.
  4. We then update your order status in our system based on this notification.

This process ensures that your most sensitive financial data is handled with the highest level of security, in compliance with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS).

8. Technology, Cookies, and Analytics

8.1. Our Use of Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use them sparingly and only for essential functionality.

  • Session Cookies: These are essential for the website to function. For example, they keep track of the items in your shopping cart and keep you logged in as you navigate the site. These are temporary and are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Preference Cookies: We may use cookies to remember certain choices you make (like your preferred language) so you don't have to re-select them on every visit.
As stated before, we do not use cookies for tracking you across the web or for targeted advertising. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings, but be aware that disabling essential cookies may impact the functionality of our website.

8.2. Analytics

  • Google Analytics: We may occasionally use Google Analytics to gather aggregated and anonymized website traffic data. This helps us understand user behavior (e.g., which pages are most visited, how users find our site) in a way that does not personally identify you. This information is used solely to improve our website's performance and user experience. We have configured our Google Analytics implementation to respect user privacy.

8.3. Social Media Integration

  • Our website may contain links to our social media pages (e.g., Facebook) or embed content (e.g., YouTube videos). Interacting with these features is governed by the privacy policy of the respective social media company, not this one.

9. Data Security: Our Commitment to Protecting Your Information

We take our responsibility to protect your information very seriously. We have implemented a multi-layered security strategy that includes technical, administrative, and physical safeguards designed to protect your Personal Information from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

9.1. Technical Measures

  • Encryption in Transit: Our website uses Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) / Transport Layer Security (TLS) to encrypt all data transmitted between your browser and our servers.
  • Encryption at Rest: Your password is not stored in a readable format. It is processed with a strong, one-way cryptographic hashing algorithm (including a "salt") before being stored in our database.
  • Firewalls and Network Security: Our servers are protected by industry-standard firewalls and network security configurations to prevent unauthorized access.

9.2. Organizational Measures

  • Access Control: Access to Personal Information is strictly limited to authorized RASA RAHASA SDS personnel who have a legitimate business need to access it.
  • Data Protection Policies: We maintain internal policies and procedures to ensure that your data is handled consistently and securely.
  • Incident Response Plan: In the event of a data breach, we have a plan in place to promptly assess the situation, mitigate the impact, and notify affected individuals and relevant authorities as required by South Korean law.

9.3. Your Responsibility

While we do our part, security is a shared responsibility. We urge you to take steps to protect your account, such as using a strong, unique password and logging out after using a shared computer.

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

In accordance with South Korea's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) and global best practices, we recognize and uphold your rights over your data. You have the full ability to exercise these rights at any time.

  • The Right to Access: You have the right to request a copy of the Personal Information we hold about you.
  • The Right to Rectification: You have the right to request the correction of any inaccurate or incomplete Personal Information.
  • The Right to Erasure (The "Right to be Forgotten"): You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated Personal Information, subject to our legal and contractual retention obligations.
  • The Right to Withdraw Consent: Where we rely on consent for processing, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time.
  • The Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority in South Korea if you believe your privacy rights have been violated.

How to Exercise Your Rights:

You can exercise these rights easily through the following methods:

  • Online Account Dashboard: The easiest way to view and update your information is by logging into your account on our website.
  • Contacting Us Directly: For requests such as account deletion or a full data export, please contact us via email or phone using the details in the "Contact Us" section below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe as stipulated by law. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

11. Data Retention: How Long We Keep Your Information

We retain your Personal Information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

  • Account Information: We retain your account profile information for as long as your account remains active.
  • Transaction Information: We are required by South Korean law to retain records of commercial transactions for a period of 5 years.
  • After Account Deletion: If you request to delete your account, we will erase your personal profile information. However, we will retain anonymized transaction data and any information required for our legal obligations.

When the retention period expires, we will securely delete or anonymize your Personal Information so that it can no longer be associated with you.
