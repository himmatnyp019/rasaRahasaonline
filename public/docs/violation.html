2. Violation Classification System

We classify violations into three categories based on their severity and impact on our platform and community:

2.1 Minor Violations

MINOR Definition: Less serious infractions that disrupt platform experience but don't pose significant security risks.

Examples Include:

  • Fraudulent Names: Using fake names or impersonating others
  • Inappropriate Language: Using bad words or mild offensive language
  • Minor Spam: Posting repetitive content or minor promotional violations
  • Profile Misuse: Using inappropriate but non-explicit profile information
  • Order Manipulation: Minor attempts to manipulate order processes
Penalty: Temporary suspension for 1 day

2.2 Moderate Violations

MODERATE Definition: More serious infractions that significantly impact user experience and platform integrity.

Examples Include:

  • Rude Communication: Using rude, disrespectful, or offensive words in chat boxes
  • Inappropriate Reviews: Posting rude, offensive, or inappropriate content in review areas
  • Harassment: Moderate level harassment of other users or staff
  • Content Violations: Posting inappropriate content that doesn't meet severe criteria
  • Platform Misuse: Misusing platform features in ways that affect other users
  • Repeated Minor Violations: Multiple minor violations after warnings

Immediate Actions:

  • Immediate warning issued
  • Content removal - offending content is immediately deleted
  • Content posting restrictions may be applied
Penalty: Temporary suspension for 1 week

2.3 Severe Violations

SEVERE Definition: Critical infractions that pose serious security risks, legal concerns, or significant harm to the platform and community.

Examples Include:

System Security Breaches:
  • Modifying internal programs or code
  • Attempting to crack authentication systems
  • Bypassing payment security measures
  • Unauthorized access attempts to user data or systems
Cyber Attacks:
  • Participating in DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service) attacks
  • Data cracking or hacking attempts
  • Malware distribution or system exploitation
Serious Harassment:
  • Harmful, threatening, or harassing content on profiles
  • Severe harassment in chat communications
  • Threatening or harmful content in reviews
  • Sexual harassment or explicit content
  • Threatening behavior toward other users or staff
Penalty: Permanent account termination (no warnings given)

3. Warning System

3.1 Warning Process

  • Three-Strike System: Users receive up to 3 warnings before account suspension
  • Warning Accumulation: Warnings accumulate across all violation types (except severe)
  • Warning Reset: Warnings may reset after 6 months of good behavior
  • Immediate Action: Severe violations result in immediate permanent termination without warnings

3.2 Warning Notifications

Users are notified of warnings through:

  • Email notification to registered email address
  • Direct message within the platform
  • Pop-up dialog box when logging into account
  • Help Center notification accessible from user dashboard

3.3 Warning Details

Each warning includes:

  • Specific violation description
  • Evidence of the violation (when applicable)
  • Policy reference that was violated
  • Consequence of continued violations
  • Appeal information and process
  • Improvement guidance for future conduct

4. Penalty System and Enforcement

4.1 Suspension Types

Minor Violation Suspension (1 Day):

  • Account Access: Completely restricted
  • Duration: 24 hours from violation time
  • Features Affected: All platform features inaccessible
  • Orders: Existing orders continue processing
  • Communication: User notified via email and login dialog

Moderate Violation Suspension (1 Week):

  • Account Access: Completely restricted
  • Duration: 7 days from violation time
  • Features Affected: All platform features inaccessible
  • Orders: Existing orders continue processing
  • Communication: User notified via email and login dialog
  • Content: Violating content permanently removed

Severe Violation Termination (Permanent):

  • Account Access: Permanently disabled
  • Duration: Indefinite
  • Features Affected: Complete platform ban
  • Orders: Current orders cancelled (refunds processed)
  • Data: Account data retained for legal/security purposes
  • Communication: User notified via email and login dialog

4.2 Content Removal

  • Immediate Removal: Violating content is removed immediately upon detection
  • No Recovery: Removed content cannot be restored
  • Backup: We may retain copies for investigation and appeals
  • Notification: Users are informed about content removal

6. Appeal Process

6.1 Appeal Eligibility

Who Can Appeal:

  • Users with minor violation suspensions
  • Users with moderate violation suspensions
  • Users with severe violation terminations
  • No Restrictions: All users can appeal regardless of violation type

When to Appeal:

  • Immediate: Appeals can be submitted immediately after penalty
  • Time Limit: Appeals accepted up to 30 days after penalty
  • Multiple Appeals: Users may submit one appeal per violation

6.2 Appeal Submission Process

Access Method:

  • Help Center: Available to all users including banned accounts
  • Dedicated Section: Special appeal section for suspended/terminated users
  • Login Alternative: Banned users can access Help Center without logging in

Required Information:

  • Account Details: Username, email, and phone number
  • Violation Reference: Violation ID or date of penalty
  • Appeal Reason: Detailed explanation of why the penalty should be reconsidered
  • Evidence: Any supporting evidence or documentation
  • Contact Method: Preferred method for appeal response

Submission Methods:

  • Online Form: Through Help Center appeal form
  • Email: Send appeal to sdsrasarahasa77@gmail.com with subject "APPEAL - [Username]"
  • Phone: Call support lines for urgent appeal discussions

6.3 Appeal Review Process

Review Timeline:

  • Initial Review: 3-5 business days for initial assessment
  • Detailed Investigation: Up to 10 business days for complex cases
  • Severe Cases: May require additional time for thorough investigation
  • Emergency Appeals: Urgent cases reviewed within 24 hours

Review Criteria:

  • Evidence Accuracy: Verification of original violation evidence
  • Context Analysis: Consideration of circumstances surrounding violation
  • User History: Review of user's overall platform conduct
  • Policy Application: Ensuring correct policy interpretation and application
  • Technical Issues: Investigation of potential system errors

Appeal Outcomes:

  • Appeal Approved: Penalty removed, account restored, apology issued
  • Appeal Partially Approved: Penalty reduced (e.g., permanent to temporary)
  • Appeal Denied: Original penalty maintained, additional explanation provided
  • Appeal Under Review: More time needed for investigation
